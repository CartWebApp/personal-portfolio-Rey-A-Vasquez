Education is Power!

CART

As mentioned previously, everything that I learned about Web Development stemmed from a CTE program I took in Clovis, CA: the Center for Advanced Research and Technology. I attend the program every Monday-Friday, from 7:45am-10:45am.

Edison High

However, my home school is Edison High School located in the south region of Fresno, CA. There I take numerous advanced classes such as AP Calculus AB, AP Chemistry, and AP US History in the afternoon.

My senior year, I plan to take AP Calculus BC, AP Physics C: Mechanics, AP Gov & Econ, and AP Statistics.

Takeaways

My time at CART was priceless. I went from someone who only new basic HTML tags from 7th grade to somebody who can make their own websites!

My skill growth was explosive thanks to the numerous projects assigned, along with incorporating AI. Many people are afraid of it, but to me, it’s an incredibly useful tool to learn new skills and information. However, do not let the machine take over your critical thinking! Knowledge is power!
